About Me

Hey there! I'm Arif Woozeer, and I'm deeply passionate about integrating emotions into gaming and exploring how computing can enhance affective experiences. Currently, I'm co-founding Kura Kura while studying Computing & Affective Technology at Singapore Management University (SMU). My journey has been quite diverse, starting from my role as a Ground Response Officer with the Singapore Police Force to diving headfirst into the startup world with Kura Kura. Along the way, I've honed my skills in React Native, front-end development, data analysis, and project management. I thrive on learning and using new technologies to tackle real-world challenges. Beyond technology, I'm captivated by how it can positively transform lives, especially when combined with behavioral science. When I'm not coding or brainstorming ideas, you'll find me at the gym, engrossed in a good book, or striving to maintain my Duolingo streak. I'm also passionate about nurturing youth entrepreneurship and actively involved in organizing events and competitions to foster this spirit. If you're into tech startups, affective computing, or simply want to discuss innovative concepts, let's connect! Feel free to explore my projects and experiences, or drop me a message directly. I'm keen on mutual learning and potentially collaborating on something groundbreaking together!
