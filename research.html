Research Interests

Reach Alliance

As a member of the Reach Alliance, I've been deeply involved in researching innovative solutions to global challenges. Our focus has been on developing strategies to reach the hardest to reach populations, ensuring that no one is left behind in the pursuit of sustainable development.

Affective Technologies

My second major in Affective Technologies at Singapore Management University has opened up fascinating avenues for research. This interdisciplinary field combines computing, psychology, and neuroscience to create technologies that can recognize, interpret, and simulate human emotions.

Emotions in Technology

My research interests lie at the intersection of emotions and technology. I'm fascinated by how we can integrate emotional intelligence into digital systems to create more empathetic and effective user experiences. Some of my key areas of focus include:

  • Emotion recognition algorithms in gaming environments
  • The role of affective computing in mental health applications
  • Emotional AI in educational technology
  • Ethical considerations in emotion-aware systems

Current Projects

I'm currently working on several research projects that align with my interests in affective technologies and emotions in computing. While these projects are in various stages of development, I'm excited about their potential to push the boundaries of how we interact with technology on an emotional level.
